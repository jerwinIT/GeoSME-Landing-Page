Empowering SMEs with Smart Location Insights

GeoSME Batangas is a GIS-powered platform designed to enhance SME visibility, accessibility, and market insights in Batangas Province. We bridge businesses and consumers, driving economic growth.

Why Choose GeoSME?

Unlike standard maps, GeoSME provides actionable insights and strategic tools for SME growth, making business discovery seamless and data-driven.

Who Benefits?

  • Entrepreneur: Gain visibility, analyze trends, and identify strategic locations.
  • Consumers - Easily find and connect with businesses.

Core Features

SME Directory

  • Categorized Listings - Businesses sorted by industry, ownership, and status.
  • SME Registration - Owners can claim and manage profiles.

Business Reviews & Ratings

  • Consumer Feedback - Ratings and reviews for informed decisions.
    • Sentiment Tracking - Insights for service improvement.

Advanced Search & Insights

  • Smart Search - Filter by type, location, and competition.
  • Market Analytics - Identify business trends and opportunities.

GIS Business Intelligence

  • Heatmaps - View SME density and underserved areas.
  • Competitor Mapping - Locate market gaps.
  • Trend Analytics - Track consumer interest.

Navigation & Recommendations

  • Optimized Routes - Find the shortest path to businesses.
  • Nearby Services - Personalized business suggestions.
